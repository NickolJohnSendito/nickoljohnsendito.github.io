'' My name is Nickol John Eduard M. Sendito ''

  • 24 years old.
  • Male.
  • Born and raised from Brgy. Calasaguen, Brooke's Point, Palawan.
  • Currently studying 3rd Year on BS-Information and technology in Palawan State University Main campus.

           I was born in a simple town in Brooke's Point, I am the eldest son of Mr. and Mrs. Eduardo Sendito. When I was a child I was always present in every activities and included in the top students that rached the highest grades in our school. I was a happy and very energetic kid but one day everything has changed when I became a victim of bullying.
           After those hardships, my life has changed. I became a prisoner of my hatred and vengence. I wanted to make them feel what I felt when they were bullying and embarrassing me in front of many people. So on that day I decided to changed my personality and the way I dreased the same as how they look and behave.



Me when I was in High School

           This is me when I was in high school, probably a grade 10 student. Through all the hardships that I faced because of bullying then suddenly my parents got separated too and at that very moment I realized that my life has ruined. I started to join gangs and groups that are popular trying to find my place and the love that I am longing. I also engaged in various vices, met new friends and stopped studying several times, those actions made my life and my relationship to my family more complicated.



Me when I entered Grade 11.

           But when I Entered Senior High School and transfered to other school I've became separated with my peer group and also became friends with other people who has a better outlook towards life.



Me when I am gradually falling in love with learning again

           I realized my mistakes then and all the sacrifices that my parents did to me so to make up with them I tried to study hard and be with my new friends who are serious on finishing their studies. My friends introduced me to God and I realized that everybody is equal in the eyes of the Lord and at that very moment I became confident of who I am.



God has touched my soul

           I let God enter my life and it feels great. I thought no one ever wanted to listen to my problems, but God is always there to save me, to lift me up to the burdens that I cary and to be with me through good days and bad.



I studied in college

           Even if my parents cannot afford to make me study in college I still find ways so that I could continue to study again. I worked on my uncles restaurant to support my financials and applied to the university as a student aide. Life is so hard when you dont have diploma these days and now I had many dreams to fulfill.



And then I met her again

           The girl that I've been praying to God for so long, the girl who made a huge impact on my life in making me realize the right and wrong, the girl who love me for who I am before has returned into my life again. And right at this moment I will never ever waste the time and the opportunities that God gave me and embrace all the blessings that I have including her. Now I have many reasons to live and I am always grateful because I have them in my life.
